Catering

LET THE CHEF AT SWEET CAKES AND ROSES prepare breakfast, lunch, or dinner with beverages for your next event. We will deliver to your specified time and venue. Tasting is available by appointment beforehand.

  • • Meals served buffet style or, for an extra charge, plated.

  • • Chocolate fountains and candy stations.

  • • Hosted alcoholic beverages or cash bar.
